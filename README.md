# Transnational Transfer Knowledge Graph

A static article-to-property-graph and wiki prototype based on:

Segal, A. A., & Greenspan, I. (2024). The Americanization of Israeli Conservative Civil Society: A critical community and transnational transference perspective. *Israel Studies, 29*(2), 144–169.

This project turns the article into an interactive public-facing knowledge graph and linked wiki demo. It models people, organizations, funders, publications, events, ideas, places, and transnational transfer relationships.

The demo is designed as an AI-with-human-in-the-loop portfolio prototype: AI-assisted extraction, modeling, and wiki generation were reviewed and revised through human editorial judgment.
